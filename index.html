Janglatal Panchayat Kisaan Utpadak Sangathan Sahkari Samiti Ltd — ek registered Farmer Producer Organisation hai jo Bihar ke Katihar jile mein sthaniya kisaanon ko ek chhate ke neeche laati hai.

Hamara uddeshy hai ki har kisan ko uski upaj ka uchit muavza mile aur wo seedha bazar se jud sake. Hum prakritik kheti ko badhaawa dete hain aur Amrittulya brand ke naam se shuddh utpad taiyaar karte hain.
